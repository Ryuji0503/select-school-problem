\[=(3x-2y)(3x+2y)\]
\[=((\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})-(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}))((\sqrt{3}+\sqrt{2})+(\sqrt{3}-\sqrt{2}))\]
\[=4\sqrt{6}\]
